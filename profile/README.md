## 👋 Hey, we're Aqtivite!

We're in love with cities. With the moments that bring people together. With that little spark of "wait — I have to go to that." Aqtivite is built for exactly that: **a discovery platform that brings everything happening in your city into one place.**

> 🔥 *"Live life to the fullest with Aqtivite."*

<br />

## 💡 The story

Cities are full of life — concerts, workshops, exhibitions, community meetups, hidden cafés, weekend markets… But most of it stays **invisible unless you happen to know the right people.** Word-of-mouth is great, but it shouldn't be the only way to discover what's happening around you.

So we set out to give it the home it deserves. The whole city, on one screen.

<br />

## 🚀 Where we are today

Aqtivite is a growing discovery platform for **events, places, gatherings, and meetups**, with a native experience on web, iOS, and Android. We're focused on Turkish cities first — from headline events to the small, neighborhood gems that make a city feel alive.

<br />

## ❤️‍🔥 What we believe in

Cities belong to the people who live in them. Discovery should be **effortless**, organizing should be **empowering**, and showing up to something new should feel like the **default — not the exception.** We build with that in mind, every release.
